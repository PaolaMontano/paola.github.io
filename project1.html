StarGazing State Parks

Go to Epic Places and Explore

Michaux State Forest

85,500 acres of mountain that you can explore. Also these are working forest you will see the beautiful scenery around you. There are many activities available like hiking, hunting and mountain biking.

Raystown Lake

There are 400 acres of waterfront property. There are camping sites that you can enjoy and go with family or friends. You are able to fish, camp, and there are other endless activies available as well.

Cherry Springs State Park

This is an 82 acre state park which is open everyday of the year. You can enjoy the night by looking up at the stars while staying the night. The activities that can be done there are hunting, stargazing, picnicking and hiking.

Susquehannock State Forest

This is an 265,000 acre park that has lots of beautiful places to see. When visiting or staying the night to camp you can enjoy lots of outdoor activities. You can go horseback riding, fishing, and you can even go geocaching.
